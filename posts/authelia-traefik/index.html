Posted Jun 5, 2021 Updated Feb 22, 2023

Authelia is an open source Single Sign On and 2FA companion for reverse proxies. It helps you secure your endpoints with single factor and 2 factor auth. It works with Nginx, Traefik, and HA proxy.
